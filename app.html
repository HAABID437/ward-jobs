About Axonal
Shift task manager for clinicians
What is Axonal?
Axonal is a lightweight shift task manager designed for resident doctors. It helps you track patient jobs by urgency, manage your workload during a busy ward shift, and hand over outstanding tasks to incoming colleagues quickly and safely.
Information governance
✓Guest mode: no data leaves your device. Without an account, all task data is stored locally in your browser only. Nothing is transmitted to any server.
✓Signed-in mode: tasks sync via secure cloud storage. When signed in with an NHS account, your tasks are encrypted in transit and stored on Google Firebase (EU-West) to enable cross-device sync. Sign-up is restricted to @nhs.net addresses only.
✓Team sessions use temporary shared cloud storage. When you join a team session, job descriptions and patient identifiers are stored on Firebase (EU-West) for the duration of the session only. Data is automatically deleted after 12 hours.
✓No patient identifiable information required. Use only bed numbers, ward locations, or partial identifiers — never full names, dates of birth, or NHS numbers. This is especially important in team sessions and signed-in mode where data may be stored in the cloud.
✓Automatic data wipe. Personal tasks clear after 16 hours. Team session data clears after 12 hours. Signing out removes cloud sync but does not delete stored cloud data — delete your account to remove all data permanently.
✓Access controls. Your synced tasks are protected by Firebase authentication and are only accessible to your account. Team sessions are access-controlled by a 6-character code shared only with your ward team.
Best practice reminders
Use minimum necessary identifiers (e.g. Bed 7, Ward 12) rather than patient names. Do not record sensitive clinical details beyond what is needed to action the job. If your device is shared, use the End shift button to clear all data before handing over the device.
