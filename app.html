About Axonal
Shift task manager for clinicians
What is Axonal?
Axonal is a lightweight shift task manager designed for resident doctors. It helps you track patient jobs by urgency, manage your workload during a busy ward shift, and hand over outstanding tasks to incoming colleagues quickly and safely.
Information governance
✓Solo mode: no data leaves your device. In normal use, all task data is stored locally in your browser only. Nothing is transmitted to any server.
✓Team sessions use temporary cloud storage. When you join a team session, job descriptions and patient identifiers are stored on a secure server (Google Firebase, EU-West) for the duration of the session only. Data is automatically deleted after 12 hours.
✓No patient identifiable information required. Use only bed numbers, ward locations, or partial identifiers — never full names or NHS numbers. This applies especially in team sessions where data is shared across devices.
✓Automatic data wipe. Personal tasks clear after 16 hours. Team session data clears after 12 hours. No residual patient information persists beyond the shift window.
✓Team sessions are access-controlled by code. Only colleagues with the 6-character session code can join and view the shared job list.
Best practice reminders
Use minimum necessary identifiers (e.g. Bed 7, Ward 12) rather than patient names. Do not record sensitive clinical details beyond what is needed to action the job. If your device is shared, use the End shift button to clear all data before handing over the device.
